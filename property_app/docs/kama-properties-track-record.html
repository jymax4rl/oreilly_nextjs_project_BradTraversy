Executive summary. Kama Properties is a production-ready short-stay rental platform with verified listings, real-time availability, dynamic pricing, secure checkout, automated booking confirmation emails, and separate experiences for guests, hosts, and admins — positioned to scale across West Africa and beyond.

1. Product vision & market potential

The platform addresses a growing demand for trusted, bookable vacation stays in markets where inventory is fragmented and payments cross borders. By combining discovery, messaging, calendar control, and card/mobile money checkout in one product, Kama Properties can capture both leisure travelers and diaspora visitors booking stays for family and events.

Guests

Browse listings, save favorites, book with transparent fees, receive branded confirmation emails, and manage trips from My Bookings.

Hosts

List properties, set nightly/weekly/monthly rates, block dates, apply custom day pricing, and get notified on every new reservation.

Platform

Admin moderation, transaction ledger, booking repair tools, and SEO-ready property pages on a verified domain.

Revenue levers

Service fee (7%) + cleaning fee (15% of stay base), multi-currency display (XOF, USD), and future subscription tiers for hosts.

4. Booking & email flow (end-to-end)

1. Guest selects dates on listing → availability API validates stay nights.
2. Checkout shows stay total + 15% cleaning + 7% service fee in local currency.
3. Flutterwave payment succeeds → transaction saved → confirmed booking created (idempotent on transaction ID).
4. Branded emails sent to guest and host with property image, location, stay window, amount, and dashboard CTA.
5. Calendar blocks booked nights; host sees reservation in My Listings.

5. Growth & roadmap potential

  • Geographic expansion — duplicate playbooks for Senegal, Ghana, Côte d’Ivoire with localized payment rails.
  • Host analytics — occupancy, ADR, and revenue dashboards from existing booking + transaction data.

  • Instant Book vs Request — host-configurable booking modes on top of calendar API.

  • B2B — property managers with multi-listing calendars and team roles.
